---
title: Sign Up
---

1. Visit https://app.babba.ge/sign-up to sign up to a Babbage account. 
<img src="assets/dashboard/sign-up.png"/>
2. Enter your email address and password (you may need to validate your email
   address). Or use one of the third party account providers (Google or GitHub) to create your account.

* You can also access the Sign Up page through the Sign In page, which the dashboard redirects you
   to in case you are not already logged in.*
