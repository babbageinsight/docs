---
title: Sign Up
---

1. Visit https://app.babba.ge/sign-up to sign up for a Babbage Insight account. 
<img src="/assets/dashboard/sign-up.png"/>
2. Enter your email address and password (you may need to validate your email
   address). Or use one of the third party account providers (e.g. Google or GitHub) to create your account.

*HINT: You can also access the Sign Up page via the [Sign In](/auth/sign-in) page, which https://app.babba.age/ redirects you to in case you are not already logged in.*
