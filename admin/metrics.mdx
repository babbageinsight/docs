# Safe Zone

1. Visit the Metric Administration page.
<img src="/assets/dashboard/metric-admin-menu.png"/>

## Add a Metric

1. Click on 'Add New' on the top right corner.
2. Fill in the Friendly Name of the metric and the SQL to pull it from your data
   source, then hit "Update Metric".
<img src="/assets/dashboard/add-metric-detail.png"/>


## Update a Metric
1. Click on the ellipsis / kebab menu next to the metric you want to update,
   then Click "Update".
<img src="/assets/dashboard/update-metric.png"/>
2. Update the Friendly Name of the metric and the SQL to pull it from your data
   source, then hit "Update Metric".
<img src="/assets/dashboard/update-metric-detail.png"/>


# DANGER ZONE
## Delete a Metric
1. Click on the ellipsis / kebab menu next to the metric you want to update,
   then Click "Delete".
<img src="/assets/dashboard/update-metric.png"/>
2. Confirm your decision, and the metric will be deleted.
<img src="/assets/dashboard/confirm-delete.png"/>
